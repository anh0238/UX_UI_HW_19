The CivicHelper Mobile Application:

The easy way to discover, schedule, and manage volunteer opportunities

Project Scope: Timeline: Identify and solve a need in users’ everyday lives by researching, prototyping, testing, and validating an idea for a brand-new mobile application.

The Problem: Looking for volunteer opportunities can be difficult and time consuming.

The Solution: Use CivicHelper, which eliminates the time a user has to spend on researching and finding opportunities, so that their time can be used to actively volunteer, to increase the amount of volunteering in a given community.
